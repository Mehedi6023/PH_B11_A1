Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the creation of new ideas, solutions, or products that drive progress and transformation.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting current needs without compromising future generations' promoting balance and resilience.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure encompasses essential systems, functioning, including transportation, utilities, communication, and public services.

Empowerment

Empowerment is enabling individuals or communities to gain confidence, skills, and authority to achieve their goals independently.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh has limitless potential to lead the world in innovation and sustainability. Our youth are brimming with creativity and the power to shape a future free of poverty, unemployment, and carbon emissions. By embracing social business, gender equality, and renewable energy, we can ensure shared prosperity for all. Bangladesh must prioritize education, technology, and environmental stewardship to overcome challenges and seize opportunities. Together, we can build a nation that thrives on inclusivity, dignity, and human creativity. Let us lead by example, showing the world how a small country can achieve greatness through vision, collaboration, and unwavering determination."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
