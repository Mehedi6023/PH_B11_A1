Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Sustainability is the practice of meeting current needs without compromising future generations' promoting balance and resilience.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure encompasses essential systems, functioning, including transportation, utilities, communication, and public services.

Empowerment is enabling individuals or communities to gain confidence, skills, and authority to achieve their goals independently.
